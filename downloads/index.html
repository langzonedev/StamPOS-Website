Downloads

No public production installer is available.

StamPOS 1.0.0 is currently distributed through controlled validation. Executables are supplied only to approved testers under written terms for named devices, builds and testing periods.

Controlled access

Controlled 1.0.0 builds

Controlled 1.0.0 builds are used for approved validation, demonstrations and pilot preparation. They may still be gated, replaced or withdrawn while deployment controls are finalised.

Potential testers can enquire at stampos@outlook.com.

Terms required

Tester approval

Before receiving an installer, testers may be required to sign a Controlled Pilot EULA and Pilot Testing Agreement, identify approved devices and confirm testing safeguards.

Sandbox first

Payment restrictions

Validation builds use simulator, sandbox or specifically approved live-pilot payment modes. Merchant onboarding, terminal supply and production credentials remain separate deployment requirements.

Planned

Public installer availability

Public installer access will be published only after controlled validation, hardware checks, documentation, security review and release gates are complete.

Safety

Verify downloads
