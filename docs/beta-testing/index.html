Controlled testing

Pilot testing documents and operating controls.

These materials describe how StamPOS intends to control validation builds, tester access, payment testing, incidents and feedback. Final legal documents will be reviewed and issued directly to each approved tester.

Core testing rules

Controlled validation builds are not for general production use unless a specific live pilot is authorised in writing. Sandbox or simulator payment modes are required for ordinary testing. Testers must maintain independent records and backups, minimise personal information and immediately stop affected testing if money, payment state, security or data integrity may be at risk.

Legal review

The EULA and Pilot Testing Agreement are working templates and are not presented as final legal advice. StamPOS intends to have the documents reviewed by an Australian commercial solicitor before relying on them with unrelated external businesses or live commercial transactions.

Document control

Each issued document should identify its version, effective date, approved build, tester, devices and testing period. Website summaries do not replace the signed documents supplied for a particular controlled pilot.
