Brand system

Practical. Reliable. Built for the counter.

StamPOS should feel modern and commercial without losing its purpose: helping operators complete sales quickly, safely and with less stress.

Core idea

A practical POS that gets out of the operator's way.

The brand should avoid vague SaaS language. Every visual and sentence should support speed, clarity, offline confidence and real-world deployment.

Colour palette

Navy
#13283AGreen
#19A974Coral

Voice

Use plain, confident wording. Prefer “Works offline”, “Large touch targets”, “Unknown is not declined”, and “Technician tools included” over hype.

Design rules

Lead with screenshots and real workflows. Use dark product sections, bright sales tiles, green primary actions, rounded panels and concise operator-focused text.
