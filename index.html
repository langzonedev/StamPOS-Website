Flexible payment connectivity

A provider-neutral payment layer, with Linkly as the first production path.

StamPOS is being built around Linkly Cloud REST so merchants can use compatible acquiring and terminal options available through the Linkly ecosystem. Core POS data remains local; internet connectivity is required when a card transaction needs to reach the payment service.

1Create and persist the saleThe POS records the attempt locally
2Send to LinklyThe terminal handles card presentation and authorisation
3Record the outcomeApproved, declined, cancelled or recovery required
4Receipt and reconcileStore references and complete the operator workflow
